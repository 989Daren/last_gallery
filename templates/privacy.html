Privacy Policy

Effective March 19, 2026

The Last Gallery ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

When you upload artwork or make a purchase, we collect:

  • Email address — used to send your edit code and purchase confirmations
  • Artist name and artwork metadata — title, year, medium, dimensions, edition info, sale preferences, and optional contact information you choose to provide
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details. We retain the Stripe transaction ID for our records.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't use cookies
  • We don't use analytics or tracking services
  • We don't log or store your IP address
  • We don't collect passwords — there are no user accounts
  • We don't sell, share, or trade your personal information

How We Use Your Information

  • Email — to deliver your edit code, purchase confirmations, and shuffle notifications related to your artwork. We use Resend to send emails.
  • Artist name and metadata — displayed publicly on the gallery wall alongside your artwork, as you submitted it
  • Payment records — kept as an audit trail for completed transactions

Data Storage and Retention

Your artwork and associated metadata are intended to remain on the gallery permanently — the gallery is designed as a time capsule. Uploaded images are stored on our servers with randomized filenames.

If you upload additional artwork without purchasing an unlock within 24 hours, that artwork and its associated data are automatically removed.

Local Storage

We store your edit code in your browser's local storage so you don't have to re-enter it each visit. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use two third-party services:

  • Stripe — for payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — for transactional emails (Resend Privacy Policy)

We do not use advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics services.

Your Choices

  • All metadata fields beyond artist name and title are optional — share only what you're comfortable with
  • You can edit your artwork metadata at any time using your edit code
  • To request removal of your artwork or personal information, contact us at admin@thelastgallery.com

Children's Privacy

The Last Gallery is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe's secure infrastructure. Administrative functions are protected by PIN authentication with rate limiting.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Email admin@thelastgallery.com.
