What is the Artwork Shuffle?

The Last Gallery shuffles all artwork weekly. When the countdown reaches zero, every piece is randomly reassigned to a new tile position. Some works remain the same size, while others rise into larger, more prominent placements.

But the shuffling won't last forever. When the gallery is completely full, it stops. No new art is accepted. Every existing piece is permanently frozen in place — a time capsule of creative work.

Important...

Know this: Your creative work is about to become part of a finite collection—a living time capsule of creative works that will endure for the life of the gallery. Once every tile is filled, weekly artwork shuffling ceases and all submissions are frozen in place.

Because of this, edits are intentionally minimal: the artist and artwork text may be modified, but the image itself becomes preserved, not revisable. However, if your image does not display in the gallery as intended, send a message to admin@thelastgallery.com explaining the problem. We will then delete the entire submission and allow you to try again. One week after your last submission attempt, this option will no longer be available.

It's a rare opportunity to leave a definitive mark and take part in a closed time capsule of creative works that, once frozen, can never be expanded… only experienced.

TLG — A Human Centric Gallery

Whether it's painting, drawing, photography, collage, digital (Procreate, Krita, etc.), or mixed media, The Last Gallery honors human touch in the creative process. A brushstroke, a blended pixel, a carefully framed photograph—these are the marks of an artist at work.

We believe AI can be used as a tool. You might use it in a collage, or as a reference—just as artists have long used photographs. That's fine. What matters is that a human is physically involved in shaping the final image.

The Last Gallery reserves the right to remove raw, unedited AI-generated images, or any other content deemed inappropriate from the gallery wall.

How it All Works

1. Upload your artwork (It's Free!) — Your art will be added at the "locked" XS (extra small) tile level. It is free to shuffle, but needs to be unlocked by you to move to the next size level.

2. Unlock your artwork — This is the next step so your artwork can shuffle into larger tile sizes. The bigger the tile, the more your artwork stands out on the gallery wall.

3. Every week, the gallery shuffles — All artwork is randomly reassigned to new tiles. Your unlocked artwork may find itself in a Small, Medium, or even a Large tile!

4. Lock in your size — If you land in a larger tile, you can choose to upgrade to that tile level, ensuring your artwork will never fall below that size during future shuffles.

Important: When the gallery is completely full, no new art will be accepted and every existing piece will be permanently frozen in place. At that point, the Gallery has become a time capsule of creative work. On the day of the final random shuffle, the tile size you've upgraded to is where it stays for the life of The Last Gallery.
