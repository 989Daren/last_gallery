Important...

Know this: Your creative work is about to become part of a finite collection—a living time capsule of creative works that will endure for the life of the gallery. Once every tile is filled, weekly artwork shuffling ceases and all submissions are frozen in place.

Because of this, edits are intentionally minimal: the artist and artwork text may be modified, but the image itself becomes preserved, not revisable. However, if your image does not display in the gallery as intended, send a message to admin@thelastgallery.com explaining the problem. We will then delete the entire submission and allow you to try again. One week after your last submission attempt, this option will no longer be available.

It's a rare opportunity to leave a definitive mark and take part in a closed time capsule of creative works that, once frozen, can never be expanded… only experienced.

TLG — A Human Centric Gallery

Art made by human hands, not algorithms.

🖌️
Every Medium Welcome Painting, drawing, photography, collage, digital (Procreate, Krita, etc.), or mixed media — what matters is a human touch in shaping the final image.
🤖️
AI as a Tool, Not the Artist Use AI in a collage or as a reference — just as artists have long used photographs. What counts is that a person is physically involved in the creative process.
🛡️
Quality Standards The Last Gallery reserves the right to remove raw, unedited AI-generated images or any content deemed inappropriate or low-effort.

How it All Works

An art gallery shuffling into history!

🎨️
Upload Your Artwork — It's Free! Your artwork joins a one-of-a-kind shuffling gallery. Space is very limited — once every tile is filled, uploads close forever.
🔓️
Unlock for Impact! Unlocked artwork can land in larger tiles during the weekly shuffle, giving your art more wall space and more eyes on your work.
📏️
Lock In Your Size If your artwork lands in a larger tile during a shuffle, you can upgrade to lock in that size — it'll never shrink below it again (but can still get larger).

When every tile is full, shuffling stops permanently and the gallery freezes — a time capsule of creative work. The size your artwork holds on that final day is what visitors will see for the life of The Last Gallery.
